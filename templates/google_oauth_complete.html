Google OAuth Configuration

Register Google OAuth in Django Admin

Now that you have your Google OAuth credentials, follow these steps to configure them in your Django admin:

  1. Go to Add Social Application in your Django admin
  2. Fill in the form with these values:
    • Provider: Google
    • Name: Google
    • Client ID: (paste your Google Client ID here)
    • Secret Key: (paste your Google Client Secret here)
    • Key: (leave blank)
    • Sites: Add your site (usually "127.0.0.1:8000") to the chosen sites
  3. Click "Save"

Update Your Templates

After completing the configuration, you'll need to update your login and register templates:

  1. Open templates/recipes/login.html and templates/recipes/register.html
  2. Replace the current Google button sections with the proper OAuth links: 
    
<!-- In login.html -->
<a href="{% templatetag openblock %} provider_login_url 'google' {% templatetag closeblock %}" class="btn btn-outline-dark">
    <i class="fab fa-google me-2"></i>Sign in with Google
</a>

<!-- In register.html -->
<a href="{% templatetag openblock %} provider_login_url 'google' process='signup' {% templatetag closeblock %}" class="btn btn-outline-dark">
    <i class="fab fa-google me-2"></i>Sign up with Google
</a>
Well done! You've successfully set up Google OAuth for your Recipe Book application!
